Beef Stir Fry Recipe

Required Ingredients

  • Ground Beef 85-90%
  • Onions
  • Olive/Avocado Oil
  • Garlic
  • Red/Green/Orange Peppers
  • Stiry Fry Sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Rice
  • Salt/Pepper/Garlic Powder/Onion Powder

Steps to Success

  1. Preheat skillet on stovetop with oil
  2. Diced onions onto skillet for 10 minutes
  3. Place 1/2 pound ground beef into skillet and season
  4. Add vegetables
  5. Add stir fry sauce
  6. After 10 minutes or so, up to you, take off
  7. Dump into bowl of rice
